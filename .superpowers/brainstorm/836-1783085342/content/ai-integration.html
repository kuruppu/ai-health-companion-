How should the AI integration work?

Balancing intelligence, cost, and user experience

A

Real-time AI for Everything

📸 Take food photo → AI
💬 "What should I eat for dinner?" → AI
⚖️ Log weight → AI analyzes
🏋️ Workout recommendation → AI decides

Pros

  • Truly personalized experience
  • Contextual recommendations
  • Natural conversations
  • Adapts to your patterns in real-time

Cons

  • Highest API costs (~$30-50/month)
  • Requires constant internet
  • Slower response times
  • Battery drain
B

Smart Caching with AI Planning

Weekly AI Planning Session
Generates 7-day meal plan + workout schedule
Local Cache
Quick access to plans
AI Refinements
On-demand adjustments

Pros

  • Much lower costs (~$5-10/month)
  • Fast daily experience
  • Mostly works offline
  • Still gets AI intelligence

Cons

  • Less real-time adaptation
  • Weekly planning sessions required
  • Not as conversational
C

AI Coach with Local Autopilot

Local Algorithms
Daily tracking & calculations
AI Coach
Weekly check-ins
📊 App calculates daily calories & macros
🍽️ App suggests meals from database
💬 AI reviews progress weekly
🎯 AI adjusts strategy & motivation

Pros

  • Lowest cost (~$2-3/month)
  • Fully offline daily use
  • Fast and reliable
  • AI for high-value coaching

Cons

  • Less "magical" feeling
  • More manual meal database work
  • Not truly hands-off

Key considerations:

  • You mentioned wanting AI to make decisions instead of manual tracking
  • Over 12 months, daily AI calls add up (365 days × multiple interactions)
  • Claude API: ~$0.015/1K input tokens, ~$0.075/1K output tokens
  • Typical meal planning conversation: ~2K-5K tokens per interaction
